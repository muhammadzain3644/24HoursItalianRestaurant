ABOUT US

Since our modest beginnings in Apr 2020 with a little space in West Drayton, 24 Hours Italian Restaurant’s development has been enlivened with the energy to cook and serve solid, Italian food.
In contrast to other Italian eateries, ‘24 Hours Italian Restaurant’ was made with the explicit expectation to appear as something else.
Characteristics of our success are:

  • asty Food. Invariably the food is tasty and well prepared.
  • Good Service.
  • Hospitable Atmosphere.
  • Reasonable Cost.
  • Convenient Location.
  • Hygiene and Cleanliness.

We believe in our quality and customer satisfaction is our first priority
